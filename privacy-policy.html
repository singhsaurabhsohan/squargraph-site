Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · SQUARGRAPH™, New Delhi, India

The short version

We collect only what we need to run our work. We never sell your data. You can ask us to delete it at any time.

This policy covers what we collect, why we need it, and what we do with it. If something here isn't clear, email us at hello@squargraph.com and we'll give you a straight answer.

What we collect

Contact forms

When you reach out through squargraph.com, we collect your name, email address, phone number, company name, and the project details you choose to share. Nothing is collected in the background without your knowledge.

Brand Growth Audit™ submissions

The audit form asks for more detailed information so we can generate a meaningful Brand Growth Audit™ assessment. This includes your brand description, website URL, social media profiles, competitor information, approximate marketing budget, and business goals. This information is used solely to run your audit and to improve the accuracy of future outputs.

Discovery Session™ bookings

When you book a Discovery Session™, payment is processed by Razorpay. We receive confirmation of the transaction along with the contact details you provide at checkout. We do not store payment card information on our servers at any point.

Analytics

We use Google Tag Manager, Google Analytics, and Meta Pixel to understand how people use this site. This data is aggregated where possible and helps us make the site more useful over time.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep the site running and analytics cookies to understand traffic patterns. SQUARGRAPH™ does not set advertising cookies directly. Third-party services like Google and Meta may set their own cookies, governed by their respective privacy policies.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry or project brief
  • To run your Brand Growth Audit™ and generate your report
  • To schedule and deliver your Discovery Session™
  • To send you the materials promised at the time of purchase
  • To improve the quality of our services and this website

We don't use your contact details for unsolicited marketing, and we don't add anyone to a mailing list without explicit consent first.

Data storage

Your information is stored in Supabase, a PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS infrastructure. We take reasonable precautions to protect your data from unauthorised access, disclosure, or accidental loss.

Audit submissions are kept for the duration of your engagement and a reasonable period thereafter for internal quality review. Contact form submissions are retained for as long as there's an active or potential business relationship.

Third-party services

We work with the following services, each of which operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Razorpay: payment processing
  • Supabase: data storage
  • Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager: site analytics
  • Meta Pixel: ad performance measurement
  • Pipedream: workflow automation
  • Google Gemini: AI-assisted analysis, used only for the Brand Growth Audit™
  • Cloudflare: security and performance

We do not sell your data to any of these services or to any third party, full stop.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct anything that's inaccurate
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@squargraph.com with the subject line "Data Request". We'll respond within 14 business days.
