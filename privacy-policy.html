Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · SQUARGRAPH™ Studios, New Delhi, India

The short version

We collect only what we need to run our engagements. We never sell your data. You can ask us to delete it at any time.

This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and what we do with it. If you have questions that aren't answered here, email us at hello@squargraph.com.

What we collect

Contact forms

When you fill out a brief or contact form on squargraph.com, we collect your name, email address, phone number, company name, and the project details you choose to share.

Brand Growth Audit™ submissions

The audit form collects more detailed information to generate your Brand Quotient™ assessment. This includes your brand description, website URL, social media profiles, competitor information, marketing budget, and business goals. This information is used solely to run your audit and improve the accuracy of the output.

Discovery Session™ bookings

When you book a Discovery Session™, Razorpay processes your payment. We receive confirmation of the transaction and any contact details you provide during checkout. We do not store payment card details on our servers.

Analytics

We use Google Tag Manager, Google Analytics, and Meta Pixel to understand how people use this website. This data is aggregated and anonymised where possible. It helps us improve the site experience.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to run the site and analytics cookies to understand traffic. No advertising cookies are set directly by SQUARGRAPH™. Third-party services (Google, Meta) may set their own cookies governed by their respective privacy policies.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry or project brief
  • To run your Brand Growth Audit™ and generate your report
  • To schedule and deliver your Discovery Session™
  • To send you the materials promised at the time of purchase
  • To improve the quality of our services and website

We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing. We do not add you to mailing lists without your explicit consent.

Data storage

Your information is stored securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS infrastructure). We take reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss.

Audit submissions are retained to deliver your report and for internal quality review. Contact form submissions are retained for the duration of any business relationship and a reasonable period thereafter.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Razorpay — payment processing
  • Supabase — data storage
  • Google Analytics / Google Tag Manager — analytics
  • Meta Pixel — ad performance measurement
  • Pipedream — workflow automation
  • Google Gemini — AI-assisted analysis (audit feature only)
  • Cloudflare — security and performance

We do not sell your data to any of these services or any third party.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Request that we delete your data
  • Withdraw consent for processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@squargraph.com with the subject line "Data Request". We will respond within 14 business days.
