Professional Background

Close to the moving parts of modern brand growth.

Before founding SQUARGRAPH™, Saurabh spent more than eight years working close to brands as they moved from idea to campaign, launch to conversation, content to culture, and visibility to trust.

His work has sat at the intersection of social media, branded content, creators, PR, production, community, go-to-market communication, reputation, and public-facing brand narratives.

Across automotive launches, national-scale digital campaigns, sports-linked brand moments, creator collaborations, live activations, and reputation-sensitive communication, he saw how easily brand meaning gets diluted when every channel begins moving in its own direction.

Strong brands are not built by creating more noise. They are built by making every visible action carry the same intent.
