Silence Sells

Reimagining EV Launch Communication

Problem

EV launches in India are loud, feature-heavy, and forgettable. Everyone is saying more. No one is saying anything meaningful.

Insight

In a noisy category, silence becomes the strongest signal.

Idea

Build a campaign that removes noise instead of adding to it.

Execution

Minimal visuals, controlled lighting, copy-led storytelling, and a silent-first approach that builds intrigue before reveal.

Outcome

Designed to create recall, curiosity, and a differentiated positioning in a saturated EV market.
