What We Are Building

A focused studio with serious ambition.

SQUARGRAPH™ is being built as a focused, strategy-led creative studio where brand, digital, content, campaigns, media and growth move under one direction.

That means people are not confined to narrow delivery lanes. They are expected to understand the larger problem, contribute beyond their job title and help raise the standard of the work around them.

We are building the studio carefully, with a preference for sharp thinking, strong craft and dependable collaboration over unnecessary scale.

How We Work

Clear standards. Flexible ways of working.

Our working model is flexible and depends on the role, project stage and collaboration needs. Some work can happen remotely, while workshops, reviews and key working sessions may take place in person.

01

Strategy before execution

We begin by understanding the business problem, audience and intended outcome.

02

Focused teams, high ownership

People stay close to the work and are expected to make thoughtful decisions.

03

Direct communication

Clarity is preferred over layers, long approval chains and unnecessary meetings.

04

AI as an accelerator

We use AI to support research, exploration, prototyping and production, while judgment and accountability remain human.

05

Quality over volume

More output is not automatically better work.

06

Flexible, not vague

Work may be remote, hybrid or project-dependent, but expectations, timelines and ownership remain clear.

Hiring Process

A considered process, without unnecessary theatre.

  1. 01

    Introduction

    We review your application, portfolio and the relevance of your experience.

  2. 02

    Conversation

    A practical discussion about your work, judgment, expectations and the opportunity.

  3. 03

    Working Discussion

    Depending on the role, we may review a brief, case, portfolio or realistic work situation.

  4. 04

    Decision

    We align on role scope, working model, commercial terms and next steps.

We do not use unpaid speculative assignments for live client work. If a task is required, it must be short, relevant and clearly explained.

We aim to respond to shortlisted applicants. Due to application volume, we may not be able to reply individually to every submission.
