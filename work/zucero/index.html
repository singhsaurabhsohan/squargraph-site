Project context

An owned presence with the depth of an editorial experience.

ZUCERO is a heritage-led sweetener brand built around Khand, Jaggery and Mishri. The website introduces its product world through ritual, collection, craft, founder story and editorial content rather than reducing the experience to a conventional product catalogue.

Experience strategy

A product world revealed through sequence, not noise.

The experience builds meaning in a deliberate order. It begins with ritual, moves through the collection and its craft, then expands into founder context and editorial stories.

01

Ritual before catalogue

The opening establishes an emotional and sensory world before presenting individual products.

02

Products as stories

Khand, Jaggery and Mishri are introduced through origin, process and character rather than feature lists.

03

Continuity across screens

Typography, imagery and pacing remain legible and intentional from wide desktop surfaces to mobile.

Selected visual journey

The collection enters as a chapter, not a grid.

Editorial pacing gives each product room to establish its own material and cultural character while remaining part of one coherent brand world.

Responsive expression

One editorial rhythm across desktop and mobile.

The wide experience uses scale and spatial restraint. The mobile experience preserves the same hierarchy while allowing product imagery and narrative copy to remain clear at smaller widths.
