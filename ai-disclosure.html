AI Disclosure

Last updated: June 2026 · SQUARGRAPH™, New Delhi, India

What we use AI for

At SQUARGRAPH™, AI is a tool in our process, not a replacement for strategic judgment. The Brand Growth Audit™ and Brand Quotient™ scoring system combine automated data collection, AI-assisted analysis, and our own evaluation frameworks to produce brand assessments quickly and consistently.

Here is what happens when you run an audit:

01
Data Collection
Automated systems pull publicly available information from your website, social profiles, and the competitor URLs you provide in the audit form.
02
AI Analysis
Google Gemini processes the collected data through SQUARGRAPH™'s proprietary scoring framework across three dimensions: web presence, communication quality, and competitive positioning.
03
Brand Quotient™ Score
A weighted composite score is generated: 40% web presence, 30% communication, 30% competitive positioning. Human strategic review is available through follow-on engagements.

What AI-generated analysis can tell you

  • How your digital presence stacks up against competitors based on observable signals
  • Communication patterns and consistency across publicly accessible touchpoints
  • Structural gaps in positioning, messaging, and content
  • Directional strategic opportunities based on what's available in the data

What it cannot tell you

AI-generated insights are based on available data, analytical models, and automated systems.

They won't capture every business variable, market condition, or contextual nuance relevant to your situation. Treat these outputs as directional insights, not definitive business advice.

Specifically, the Brand Quotient™ scoring system can't account for:

  • Internal operational realities not visible in public data
  • Offline brand perception, customer sentiment, or word-of-mouth standing
  • Financial constraints or business model specifics you haven't shared
  • Strategic context that exists only inside your organisation
  • Market events or changes that occur after data collection

Our position on AI

We think AI is genuinely useful for research and pattern recognition. What it isn't is a strategist. The Brand Growth Audit™ is built to give you a fast, structured baseline, the kind of diagnostic that typically takes a strategy team several days to produce by hand. It's a starting point, not the whole answer.

Human validation and professional judgment are recommended before making business, financial, legal, branding, or operational decisions based on any AI-generated output from SQUARGRAPH™.

For decisions that matter, we recommend following up with a Discovery Session™ where a human strategist can review the outputs in the context of your specific situation.

Data usage in AI processing

Information you submit through the Brand Growth Audit™ form is used to run your assessment only. It is not used to train AI models. Your data is not shared with third-party AI providers beyond what's technically necessary to generate your report. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
