AI Disclosure

Last updated: June 2026 · SQUARGRAPH™ Studios, New Delhi, India

What we use AI for

SQUARGRAPH™ uses AI as a tool — not as a replacement for strategic judgment. The Brand Growth Audit™ and Brand Quotient™ scoring system combine automated data collection, AI-assisted analysis, and proprietary evaluation frameworks to produce preliminary brand assessments at speed and scale.

Here is what happens when you run an audit:

01
Data Collection
Automated systems retrieve publicly available information from your website, social profiles, and the competitor URLs you provide.
02
AI Analysis
Google Gemini processes the collected data against SQUARGRAPH™'s proprietary scoring framework across three dimensions: web presence, communication quality, and competitive positioning.
03
Brand Quotient™ Score
A weighted composite score is generated. The weighting is: 40% web, 30% communication, 30% competitive. Human strategic review is available through follow-on engagements.

What AI-generated analysis can tell you

  • How your digital presence compares to competitors based on observable signals
  • Communication patterns and consistency across publicly accessible touchpoints
  • Structural gaps in positioning, messaging, and content
  • Directional strategic opportunities based on available information

What it cannot tell you

AI-generated insights are based on available information, analytical models, and automated systems.

While designed to provide meaningful strategic guidance, AI-generated outputs may not capture every business variable, market condition, operational factor, or contextual nuance relevant to your situation.

Recommendations should be treated as directional insights rather than definitive business advice.

Specifically, the Brand Quotient™ scoring system cannot account for:

  • Internal operational realities not visible in public data
  • Offline brand perception, customer sentiment, or word-of-mouth standing
  • Financial constraints or business model specifics you haven't shared
  • Strategic context that exists only within your organisation
  • Real-time market events or changes occurring after data collection

Our position on AI

We believe AI is a capable research and pattern-recognition tool. It is not a strategist. The Brand Growth Audit™ is designed to give you a fast, structured baseline — the kind of diagnostic that typically takes a strategy team several days to produce manually. It is a starting point, not a final answer.

Human validation and professional judgment are recommended before making business, financial, legal, branding, or operational decisions based on any AI-generated output from SQUARGRAPH™.

For decisions of material significance, we recommend following up with a Discovery Session™ where a human strategist reviews the outputs in the context of your specific situation.

Data usage in AI processing

Information submitted through the Brand Growth Audit™ form is used to run your assessment. It is not used to train AI models. Your submitted data is not shared with third-party AI providers beyond what is necessary to generate your report. See our Privacy Policy for full details on data handling.
