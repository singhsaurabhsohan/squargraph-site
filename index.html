The Founder

Saurabh Sohan Singh

Founder & Creative Strategist

Built by understanding how brands move in the real world.

Before founding SQUARGRAPH™, Saurabh spent more than eight years close to the moving parts of modern brand growth: campaign thinking, social media, branded content, creators, PR, production, community, reputation, and go-to-market communication.

His work has sat across automotive launches, large-scale digital campaigns, sports-linked brand moments, creator collaborations, live activations, and reputation-sensitive communication, where one brand had to show up consistently across many public touchpoints.

Across that work, he saw a pattern: when every channel starts moving separately, brand meaning becomes weaker even when activity increases.

That became the starting point for SQUARGRAPH™.

Strong brands are not built by creating more noise.

They are built by making every visible action carry the same intent.

Today, Saurabh leads the studio with a focus on strategic clarity, creative discipline, market perception, and long-term brand value.

Built for brands that want clarity before scale.
