About the Studio

We are Squargraph.
A creative studio
built without compromise.

Squargraph Studios is a Delhi-based creative and social-first studio. Born from 8+ years of experience inside India's top marketing environments — including 3+ years leading Hyundai India's integrated campaigns at INNOCEAN Worldwide — we exist to deliver what agency structures rarely allow: true ownership of ideas, from brief to execution.

We don't outsource thinking. We don't deliver decks no one acts on. We build, ship, and scale — with the clarity of a boutique studio and the capability of a full-service team. Every campaign we touch gets the attention it deserves, not the attention the billing allows.
